Glossary
  1. Member: Member of ChocAn, every member has a member card
  2. Provider: Health Care Professional who provides services to ChocAn member
  3. Terminal: desined for provider, like a credit card device in a shop
  4. Fees: Every member should pay monthly fee to ChoAn
  5. Service: health services provided by ChocAn
  6. Service Code: provider check the services provided through service codes
  7. Member Number: The ID of each member of ChocAn
  8. Provider Number: Identification of each provider
  9. Report:
    1. Report sent to ChocAn Manager automatically and manually
    2. Each member who has consulted a ChocAn provider during that week receives a list of services provided to that member
    3. Each provider will get a weekly report containing the list of services he or she provided to ChocAn members
    4. A summary report send to Manager of provider. The report lists every provider to be paid that week, the number of consultations each had, and his or her total fee for that week. Finally, the total number of providers who provided services, the total number of consultations, and the overall fee total are printed.
  10. Manager
    • Manager of the provider
    • Manager of ChocAn
  11. Interactive Mode: add/remove/update member infomation
  12. Provider Directory: an alphabetically ordered list of service names and corresponding service codes and fees
